Strength
A calm YES

What is Strength?

Strength is card VIII of the Major Arcana — inner courage and gentle mastery. The figure softly closing a lion's mouth tames wild force through patience, not violence. True power comes not from suppression but from understanding and composure.

YES or NO Verdict

Strength is A calm YES. It'll work. Just don't force it — handle it with care.

Upright / Reversed

▲ UPRIGHT
Inner strength, patience, courage, gentle mastery. True power is not force — it's composure and steady handling.
▼ REVERSED
Self-doubt, succumbing to fear, loss of inner control. Suppressing emotions only builds pressure toward an outburst. Facing them is the solution.

Advice by Situation

  • LOVE Softness gets further than force here. The gentler your approach, the more things ease.
  • PEOPLE Don't suppress emotions — manage them. Composure is what keeps the relationship intact.
  • WORK The ability is already there. Tame the impatience and you'll get where you want to go.
  • STUDY Anxiety and all, you can still do this. The capacity to hold on is working in your favour.
  • MONEY Restraint matters more than ambition right now. Measured management beats emotional decisions.
  • HEALTH Steady recovery matters more than dramatic change. Slow, but clearly moving in the right direction.
  • OTHER It'll work. Just don't force it — handle it with care.
