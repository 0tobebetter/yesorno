The World
A complete YES

What is The World?

The World is card XXI of the Major Arcana — completion, achievement, and integration. A figure dancing inside a laurel wreath expresses the joy and fullness of a journey brought to its perfect close. When this card appears, you are standing at the end of a cycle. Receive it and celebrate.

YES or NO Verdict

The World is A complete YES. Yes, fully. This is the moment of completion. Receive it.

Upright / Reversed

▲ UPRIGHT
Completion, achievement, integration, a new beginning. One journey ends in wholeness and the next door opens. Take time to fully receive this moment.
▼ REVERSED
Delayed completion, loose ends, near-miss. You're almost there — don't give up now. The finishing touch is still needed.

Advice by Situation

  • LOVE A natural completion is within reach. Things are coming together in a meaningful way.
  • PEOPLE The relationship has reached a stage of wholeness. Appreciate where you are.
  • WORK A project or goal is nearing its full expression. Finish it properly.
  • STUDY Mastery is within reach. The effort has accumulated — now let it land.
  • MONEY A cycle of success is reaching its peak. Consolidate what you've built.
  • HEALTH Your wellbeing is in a state of real balance. This is the result of sustained care.
  • OTHER Yes, fully. This is the moment of completion. Receive it.
