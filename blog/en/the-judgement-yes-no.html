Judgement

What is Judgement?

Judgement is card XX of the Major Arcana — awakening, rebirth, and a calling. Figures rising from graves at the sound of an angel's trumpet represent the moment of clearing the past and waking into your true self. When this card appears, it's time to answer the inner call and move to the next level.

YES or NO Verdict

Judgement is An awakening YES. Time to face things honestly and make a clear call. What you decide now will matter.

Upright / Reversed

▲ UPRIGHT
Awakening, rebirth, reckoning, a calling. The voice within already knows the way forward. Find the courage to answer.
▼ REVERSED
Self-doubt, refusing the call, lingering regret. Knowing and still looking away is the greatest loss. This may be the last chance right now.
