The Star

What is The Star?

The Star is card XVII of the Major Arcana — hope, healing, and inspiration. A figure quietly pouring water beneath a night sky shows the calm restoration that comes after the storm. When this card appears, it's a signal that warmth and hope wait ahead — the light after a hard time.

YES or NO Verdict

The Star is YES. There's reason to hope. Move gently, keep your eyes on where you're headed.

Upright / Reversed

▲ UPRIGHT
Hope, faith, healing, inspiration. The quiet light that arrives after the darkest night. The path opens for those who didn't give up.
▼ REVERSED
Lost hope, despair, disbelief. Even when the starlight is hidden, the star is still there. It's only temporarily out of sight.

Advice by Situation

  • LOVE After difficulty comes a quiet sense of hope. This is a time to believe gently in the possibility ahead.
  • PEOPLE What felt hopeless may slowly begin to heal. Give it time and space.
  • WORK A long effort is beginning to see light. Keep your direction and trust the process.
  • STUDY Even when exhausted, there's still something worth holding on to. Small progress is still progress.
  • MONEY After a rough period, a calmer phase is approaching. Good things tend to come to those who keep going.
  • HEALTH Your body is beginning to find its rhythm again. Steady, gentle care is what sustains the recovery.
  • OTHER There's reason to hope. Move gently, keep your eyes on where you're headed.
