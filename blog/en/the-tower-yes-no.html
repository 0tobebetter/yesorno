What is The Tower?

The Tower is card XVI of the Major Arcana — sudden change and the collapse of existing structures. The image of two figures falling from a lightning-struck tower in flames is shocking, but what falls was never truly solid. When this card appears, reassessing the situation calmly matters more than pushing forward.

YES or NO Verdict

The Tower is A sharp NO. Be prepared. What's coming might be destabilising, but it clears the way for what actually needs to happen.

Upright / Reversed

▲ UPRIGHT
Sudden change, shock, collapse of existing structures. What falls was never truly solid to begin with. Real rebuilding comes after the fall.
▼ REVERSED
Avoidance of crisis, delayed collapse, fear. Trying to prevent what must fall only makes the eventual impact worse. Avoidance is not the answer.

Advice by Situation

  • LOVE Something built on a shaky foundation is close to collapse. A difficult but honest conversation is unavoidable.
  • PEOPLE The tension that's been building will need to surface. A direct confrontation may actually clear things.
  • WORK Collapse may be exactly what's needed for something better to be built. Resistance only prolongs it.
  • STUDY A system that looked solid may crack under pressure. Now is the time to audit what you're relying on.
  • MONEY A sudden loss or exposure of something hidden may be coming. Taking cover is wiser than risking more.
  • HEALTH Ignoring the warning signs your body has been sending is becoming harder. A breaking point may be near.
  • OTHER Be prepared. What's coming might be destabilising, but it clears the way for what actually needs to happen.
