What is The Moon?

The Moon is card XVIII of the Major Arcana — illusion, uncertainty, and the unconscious. A dog and wolf howling at the moon, a crayfish emerging from a pool — images of a time when the line between reality and fantasy blurs. When this card appears, what's visible isn't the whole truth, and major decisions are better deferred.

YES or NO Verdict

The Moon is Not quite — NO. Not yet. The fog hasn't lifted. Wait until things come into focus.

Upright / Reversed

▲ UPRIGHT
Illusion, fear, uncertainty, the unconscious. When things aren't what they seem, trust your deeper instincts. Wait for the fog to lift.
▼ REVERSED
Clarity emerging, truth surfacing, fear releasing. The fog is beginning to lift. Things are slowly coming into focus.

  • HEALTH Uncertainty and restlessness are part of the cycle. Don't force anything — let your body tell you what it needs.
